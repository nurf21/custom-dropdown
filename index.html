Interaction Guide

  • Click the dropdown to open/close the menu
  • Select an item to choose it and close the dropdown
  • The selected item will be highlighted when the dropdown is reopened
  • Dropdown closes automatically when an item is selected
  • Press ESC or click outside to close the dropdown
